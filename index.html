ForceDeck Firmware Updater

Update your ForceDeck devices via USB
FW v3.7.8
WebSerial not supported. Please use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.

How to Update

1
Connect a ForceDeck device via USB-C cable
2
Click Connect and select the serial port
3
Click Update Firmware — takes ~30 seconds
4
Repeat for each device (Encoder, Left, Right)
